🚨 I built a tool that automatically scans your emails for malicious links — before you even click them.

Introducing URL Threat Analyzer — a personal cybersecurity dashboard I've been building that works silently in the background to protect you from phishing, malware, and shady redirects hiding inside your inbox.

Here's what it does 👇

🔍 AUTOMATIC EMAIL SCANNING
It connects to your Gmail and automatically scans every incoming email for suspicious links. No manual work. It runs in the background and flags threats before you ever open them.

🕵️ DEEP LINK INVESTIGATION
When a suspicious URL is found, the tool doesn't just check the link — it actually visits it inside a safe, isolated browser environment and follows every redirect, hop by hop. A short link like bit.ly/xyz might secretly bounce through 5 different servers before landing on a phishing page. We catch every step.

🛡️ REAL THREAT INTELLIGENCE
Every URL and domain is cross-checked against VirusTotal and other threat intelligence sources to see if it's been flagged by the global security community.

🔐 EMAIL AUTHENTICATION CHECKS
It validates SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records to ensure the email actually came from who it says it did — putting a stop to spoofing attacks.

🧿 TRACKING PIXEL DETECTION
It identifies invisible 1x1 tracking pixels used by marketers (and hackers) to know exactly when and where you opened an email.

🤖 AI-POWERED EXPLANATIONS
Instead of just showing you a red flag, the tool uses a local AI model to explain *why* something is dangerous in plain English — "This site is impersonating a Microsoft login page to steal your credentials."

📊 SECURITY DASHBOARD
A clean, real-time dashboard shows you:
→ All scanned emails with threat severity
→ Full redirection chain visualization
→ Which Gmail accounts are connected (and their status)
→ Complete audit history of every scan

🔒 100% LOCAL & PRIVATE
The AI runs entirely on your machine. No data is sent to third-party AI services. Your emails stay private.

---

This started as a personal project to understand how phishing attacks actually work under the hood. What began as a URL tracer turned into a full email security monitoring system.

Still building and improving it — but it's already catching real threats in real emails. 🎯
